Chicken Katsu

How To Make Chicken Katsu

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below,
but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko.
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
