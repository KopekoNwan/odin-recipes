Thin-Crust Pizza Dough

How To Make Thin-Crust Pizza Dough

This delicious thin crust pizza dough recipe is so low maintenance, you can
throw it together in just 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar and water:
  • Flour
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast with sugar and water.
  2. Make the dough.
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Roll the dough into a circle.
